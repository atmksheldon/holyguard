Privacy Policy for HolyGuard

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Introduction

HolyGuard ("we," "our," or "us") operates a mobile security application for faith communities and organizations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, assigned role (admin, security, member)
  • Organization Information: Church/organization name, address, and location coordinates
  • Authentication Data: Password (encrypted), email verification status

User-Generated Content

  • Security Reports: Photos, descriptions, timestamps, and location data for incident reports
  • Team Messages: Text communications with other members of your organization

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when submitting incident reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the HolyGuard service
  • Enable real-time security alerts and team communication within your organization
  • Display your organization's location on the network map
  • Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
  • Verify email addresses for account security
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase and Google Cloud Platform
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Control: Only authenticated members of your organization can access your organization's data
  • Backups: Data is backed up regularly to prevent loss

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Within Your Organization

  • Security alerts and team messages are shared with other authenticated members of your organization
  • Your name and role are visible to other members of your organization

We Do NOT Share

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not share data between different organizations

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of HolyGuard, our users, or the public
  • Investigate and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Data: Access your profile information within the app
  • Update Data: Modify your name, email, and password
  • Delete Account: Contact us to delete your account and associated data

Location Data

You can disable location services in your device settings, but this will limit incident reporting functionality.

Communications

  • You control which team messages you send
  • You can log out at any time to stop receiving notifications

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days
  • Security Reports: Incident reports may be retained longer for safety and legal purposes
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 90 days

Children's Privacy

HolyGuard is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Services

HolyGuard uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, and file storage
  • Google Maps/Places API: Address lookup and location services
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Notifications (iOS only)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located in the United States and other countries where Google Cloud Platform operates. By using HolyGuard, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of HolyGuard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.

Consent

By using HolyGuard, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
